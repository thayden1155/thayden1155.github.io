Surfing

Surfing is a big part of my family's life when we are in Costa Rica. If possible, we surf everyday.

It really just depends on if the waves are good or not. We typically go out in the mornings because that’s when it's best.

We also love to go out and surf during sunset, it’s very beautiful and calming. It was great having my whole family out there surfing this year.

Everytime two or more of us share a wave, one of us yells “party wave!”. Surfing with family and friends is always a great time!

I had some of my favorite moments this summer just surfing with the family.
